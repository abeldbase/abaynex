How It Works

A simple idea with long-term structural value

NexMix™ is built around a self-healing concrete approach designed to support durability and improve the long-term performance of concrete systems.

01

Integrated into the concrete system

NexMix™ becomes part of the concrete performance strategy from the beginning, supporting a more advanced material system built for long-term results.

02

Cracks develop over time

Concrete structures naturally experience stress, shrinkage, loading, and environmental exposure that may lead to cracking.

03

Healing response supports crack management

The self-healing approach is designed to help reduce vulnerability associated with cracking and support stronger long-term concrete behavior.

04

Durability performance improves

With better crack response and greater resilience against deterioration, concrete systems are better positioned for long-term service and value.
